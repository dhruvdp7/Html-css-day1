At QA InfoTech (an ISO 9001:2008, 20000-1:2005, 27001:2005 and CMMI Level III certified company), we specialize in providing independent offshore software testing and unbiased software quality assurance services to product companies, ranging from the Fortune 500s to start-up companies. We are one of the reputed outsourced QA testing vendors with years of expertise helping clients across the globe.



Established in 2003, with less than five testing experts, QA InfoTech has grown leaps and bounds with three QA Centers of Excellence globally, two of which are located in the hub of IT activity in India, Noida, and the other, our affiliate QA InfoTech Inc., in Michigan, USA.



The new software testing paradigm is quite different from the traditional approach. An autonomous certified QA and software testing services organization is more collaborative and iterative; it provides customers an edge over the traditional approach. With continuous innovation in quality assurance and testing, QA InfoTech has emerged as one of the QA Thought Leaders. We are dedicated to becoming one of the best independent testing partners worldwide.
